Vijaya Babar

Judge for Mono Acting

For passes please contact,

+91 8652323878
+91 8097261674

Vijaya Babar is an elegant and glamorous Marathi actress who has worked in a lot of Marathi dramas and plays. She is an alumnus of Pillai College and was a part of 'Spotlight', the theatre group of Pillai. Being a part of Pillai College, we are pleased to see our alumni reach new heights of success. She never failed to impress everyone with her incredible acting skills during her college days. And now we are all set to welcome her as the judge for mono acting for Alegria 2022.

Vijaya started her acting career with Marathi theatre. Further, she went on to star in the music video of 'Zindagi'. She entered the Marathi TV Industry with her show 'Shikast E Ishq' in which she played a pivotal role. Currently, she is playing the role of Chanda in the ongoing tv show 'Jai Jai Swami Samarth'. She was also the winner of Miss Mumbai. Her impeccable performance has left the audience in awe. She has become an inspiration for many of us.

Vijaya is returning to her alma mater as a successful actress and we couldn't be more proud of her. She will be on campus to judge the mono acting performances and share her experiences in the acting field. With great alumni like Vijaya in our midst, it would be difficult to miss.
